Why One X Transmision for Gearbox Repair

Data-Driven Diagnostics

We don't guess. Before any gearbox work, we run a full diagnostic scan using our 7-system analyser covering 53 rules. For your gearbox specifically, we check against 8 transmission rules (TRANS_T1 through T8) including:

  • ATF temperature monitoring — should be 60–90°C at idle. Above 120°C triggers critical alert (TRANS_T2)
  • Shift time analysis — healthy shifts complete in <300ms. Slow shifts indicate clutch pack wear
  • Torque converter slip — measured at cruise speed. Must be <5% when locked (TRANS_T6)
  • Gear ratio accuracy — within ±0.5% of spec for each gear (TRANS_T7)

This tells us exactly what's wrong — so you only pay for what needs fixing.
